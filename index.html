Explore Roles in Public Service

Suggested Roles:

Role Information

Politics

Day-to-day activities in politics may involve researching policy issues, meeting with constituents, and participating in legislative sessions.

Public Service

In public service, you might work on government programs, manage public resources, and engage with the community to address their needs.

Public Relations

Manage communication between an organization and the public. This role involves creating a positive public image, handling media inquiries, and organizing events to build relationships.

Community Organizer

Facilitate and coordinate community initiatives. This role focuses on mobilizing community members, addressing local issues, and fostering collaboration to create positive social change.

Policy Analyst

Evaluate and analyze policies to provide informed recommendations. This role requires research skills, an understanding of social issues, and the ability to communicate policy implications clearly.

Diversity and Inclusion Specialist

Promote diversity and inclusion within organizations. This role involves developing strategies, training programs, and initiatives to create an inclusive environment that values diverse perspectives.
